Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book tickets?

You can easily book your tickets through our online booking system, available on our website.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 24 hours before the show. After that, the booking is non-refundable.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and UPI for all transactions.
